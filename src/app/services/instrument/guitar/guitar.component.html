Guitar

A flat-bodied stringed musical instrument with a long fretted neck and usually six strings played with a pick or with the fingers. It is played using both the hand by strum the string with either a guitar pick or the finger(fingernails) of one hand, while simultaneously pressing the strings against the frets with the fingers of the other hand. Due to the vibrating string a sound will be produced, by means of the hollow chamber of the guitar body.

There are two type of guitar used

1. electric

Electric guitar which convert vibration of string into electrical signal. The vibration occurs when we pick the string using our fingernails. he electric signal can be electronically make changes to change the timbre of the sound.

2. acoustic

An acoustic guitar is a musical instrument in the guitar type of guitar, that produces the sounds of its vibrating string acoustically through the air using the hollow part of guitar.
